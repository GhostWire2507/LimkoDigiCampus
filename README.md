# LimkoDigiCampus

A mobile app for tracking student attendance and lecturer performance at TVET colleges.

## What It Does

- Track which students attended lectures
- Let lecturers submit reports after each class
- Let students rate their lecturers
- Let programme leaders see how their courses are doing
- Let faculty managers see the big picture

## Who Uses It

- **Students** – See classes and rate lecturers
- **Lecturers** – Take attendance and submit reports  
- **Programme Leaders** – Manage courses and review reports
- **Faculty Managers** – View all faculties

## How To Use

1. Open the app on your phone or emulator
2. Login with your credentials
3. Depending on your role, you'll see different options:
   - Take attendance
   - Submit reports
   - Check ratings
   - Export data

## Built With

- Expo (React Native)
- Firebase (database)
